#  Learning path: Presentation of structured and unstructured content.
Dive into the process of retrieving and displaying various types of content, including multilingual and advanced scenarios.
Dive into the process of retrieving and displaying content with Xperience by Kentico.
As a developer, your client or organization likely depends on you to bring complex content requirements to life.
This path will guide you though the setup, retrieval, and display of various types of content in Xperience by Kentico.
## Presentation of structured and unstructured content path
  1. Developer kickstart – Starting with installation, build a bare-bones website from start to finish, including navigation and pages that display structured data.
  2. Page builder – Use Page Builder features to empower your content editors to build visual pages.
  3. Multilingual – Explore the essentials of reaching a global audience by offering multilingual content with Xperience by Kentico’s localization support.
  4. Advanced content – Take a deep dive into advanced content management and retrieval, querying content with taxonomies, reusable field schemas, and smart folders.
