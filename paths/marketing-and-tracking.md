#  Learning path: Marketing and tracking.
Set up Xperience marketing and tracking tools to help marketers drive engagement and stay compliant with privacy laws.
Explore the tracking and marketing capabilities of Xperience by Kentico, as well as the best ways to ensure your tracking complies with your region’s personal data regulations.
As a developer, your client or organization likely needs you to help them gather valuable marketing data and engage clients.
This path will guide you through the configuration and implementation of data protection, contact tracking, and member registration in your project.
## Presentation of structured and unstructured content path
  1. Data protection configuration – See how you can manage personal data or customer consents, and handle customer data and removal requests.
  2. Data protection – Implement consent management, along with collectors and erasers to make sure your project is compatible with data protection regulations.
  3. Activities and contacts – Work with contacts and their activities in Xperience by Kentico, including custom activities and contact attributes.
  4. Members – Allow visitors to register and authenticate in your website channel, manage their profile, and access special content.
