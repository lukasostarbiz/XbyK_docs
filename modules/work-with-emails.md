# Engage your audience with email
Email marketing is still one of the most powerful ways to build relationships with your audience — but only when it’s done right. In this module, you’ll learn how to set up everything you need to run successful, professional email campaigns using Xperience by Kentico.
Whether you’re welcoming new subscribers, sending out newsletters, or helping contacts manage their preferences, Xperience gives you the tools to create a smooth and consistent email experience from start to finish.
In this module, you’ll learn how to:
  * Create a recipient list and set up confirmation pages and emails for subscriptions and unsubscriptions.
  * Build a marketing email step-by-step — from subject line to design to content.
  * Send the right messages with clear, branded confirmation and unsubscription emails and pages at the right time.


By the end of this module, you’ll be ready to deliver targeted, effective emails that keep your audience informed, engaged, and in control of their preferences — all while saving time and staying compliant.
Let’s start and make your email marketing smoother, smarter, and more successful.
