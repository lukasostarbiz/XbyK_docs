When your website needs to speak more than one language, it’s not just about translating text – it’s about delivering a seamless experience to a global audience. In this module, you’ll learn how to build and manage multilingual websites using **Xperience by Kentico** localization features.
Whether you’re setting up your first language variant or adding a language selector to your UI, this module will walk you through the key steps to make your content more accessible and your implementation cleaner and easier to maintain.
## Why it matters
Adding multilingual support helps you:
  * Reach users in their preferred language
  * Improve accessibility and engagement
  * Build inclusive digital experiences that scale


With the right setup, multilingual support can be straightforward to implement.
## What you’ll learn
By the end of this module, you’ll be able to:
  * Display multilingual content on your website using language variants
  * Use Xperience’s localization features to support editors working in different languages
  * Structure multilingual URLs in a way that’s SEO-friendly and user-friendly
  * Implement a language selector control to let visitors switch languages easily
  * Configure URL behavior and routing for multilingual navigation
  * Test and troubleshoot common localization issues in your environment


Let’s get started and make your content truly global.
