# Continue learning about managing content
Great work! In this module, you’ve taken big steps toward making your content easier to manage and more effective across all your channels.
Here’s what you’ve covered:
  * **Digital marketing channels** – You learned about the digital marketing channels and what is the role of website channels in Xperience by Kentico.
  * **Reusable content** – You learned how to create content once that you can display in multiple places, save time in content curation, and reduce the risk of errors. You also know how to handle assets in the Content hub.
  * **Multilingual content** – You saw how to reach a wider audience by setting up language variants and managing translations without duplicating effort.
  * **Workflows** – You discovered how to guide your content through a clear review and approval process, so nothing gets published before it’s ready.


By combining these skills, you have learned the basics of working with content in Xperience and you can start building a content process that’s faster, more consistent, and ready to grow with your needs.
