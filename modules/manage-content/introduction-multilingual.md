Ready to make your content speak more languages? In Xperience by Kentico, creating multilingual versions of your pages and products is simple — and you’ll be in full control every step of the way.
In this section, you’ll:
  * Get a clear idea of what multilingual content variants are, see how fallback languages work, and spot if a content item already exists in other languages.
  * Set up different types of languages in Xperience and get your project ready for multilingual success.
  * Follow step-by-step instructions to build a different language version of an existing page.
  * Create a multilingual variant of a reusable product content item and keep your content consistent across languages.


By the end of this section, you’ll know exactly how to bring your content to life for audiences everywhere — quickly, confidently, and without extra hassle.
