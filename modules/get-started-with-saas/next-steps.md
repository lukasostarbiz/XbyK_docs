# What's next
Here’s a quick recap of what you learned in this module:
  * What Kentico SaaS is and how the Xperience Portal helps manage your project.
  * How to sign in to the Xperience Portal for the first time and access your project.
  * When and how to invite your (technical) team and assign them the right roles.
  * How to keep an eye on your project after deployment using logs and monitoring tools in the Xperience Portal.
