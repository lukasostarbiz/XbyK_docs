## Continue learning
Browse [our documentation](documentation/developers-and-admins/saas), which contains plenty of detailed information and useful code samples. At this point, you may find the [Differences between SaaS and private cloud solutions](documentation/developers-and-admins/saas/differences-saas-private-cloud) and the [FAQ](documentation/developers-and-admins/saas/saas-frequently-asked-questions) pages particularly helpful.
Read about **first-hand Kentico SaaS experiences of our MVPs** :
  * Lukasz Skowroński talks about the [release process considerations and deployment on the SaaS environment](https://konabos.com/blog/xperience-by-kentico-release-process-considerations-and-deployment-on-the-saas-environment).
  * Milan Lund’s blog describes his experience with [Kentico’s Xperience Portal and SaaS: From Setup to Production](https://www.milanlund.com/knowledge-base/kentico-xperience-portal-and-saas-from-setup-to-production).
  * Liam Goldfinch, in his triple-blog series, elaborates on his [migration from a self-hosted setup to our SaaS and its benefits](https://www.goldfinch.me/blog/the-kentico-saas-migration-a-step-by-step-guide), [discusses automated deployments](https://www.goldfinch.me/blog/the-kentico-saas-migration-automated-deployments) and [offers troubleshooting tips and lessons learned](https://www.goldfinch.me/blog/the-kentico-saas-migration-lessons-learned-and-troubleshooting-tips).


Outside of these resources, stay tuned for more materials. We are preparing a SaaS processes comparison guide, a deep dive into how Kentico SaaS differs from other cloud or self-managed solutions, a maintenance and troubleshooting deep dive, and more.
