Learn how to add an information to an existing contact. You can use it also for updating contact’s details.
  1. In the **Contact management** application, find the contact you want to update using the Search field. Use their first name, last name, or email address.
  2. Open the listed contact, and **Edit** the contact’s _Personal details_.
  3. Add the business phone to the contact’s profile and **Save** your changes.


You can edit any value stored in the contact’s default fields.
