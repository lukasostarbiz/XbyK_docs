# Widget personalization overview
Once you’ve collected useful information from your visitors – like names, interests, or preferences – it’s time to put that data to work. Widget personalization helps you do exactly that by tailoring the content shown on your website based on who’s viewing it.
In the following section, you’ll learn how to create personalized versions of **Page Builder** widgets for different audiences. This allows you to deliver more relevant messaging and improve the overall experience for your contacts.
