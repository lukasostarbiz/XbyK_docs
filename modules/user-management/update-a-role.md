# Update a role
Suppose a digital marketing manager in your company complains that the _Channel Managers_ should not have the right to send an email. The emails must be approved and sent only by the companyâ€™s _Email Marketers_. To update the _Digital Channel Manager_ role, follow the steps below.
**Task** :
  1. Navigate to the **Role management** application.
  2. Open the _Digital Channel Manager_ role.
  3. Go to the **Permissions** tab on the left.
  4. Find the permissions for the **Marketing Emails** channel.
  5. Remove the **Send email** permission.
  6. **Save** your changes.
