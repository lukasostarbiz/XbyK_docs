# Roles in Xperience overview
Now that you know how to create and manage individual users in **Xperience by Kentico** , it’s time to look at how to manage **which parts of the system** those users can access and **what actions** they’re allowed to perform.
That’s where **Roles** come in.
Roles help you group users by their responsibilities and assign them the right permissions for their jobs – whether they’re content editors, marketers, or data protection officers.
In the following section, you’ll learn how to create roles, assign and update permissions, and keep your team’s access structured, secure, and easy to manage.
