# User management - what's next?
You’ve just completed the **User managemen** t module, and you’re ready to manage users with confidence. Great work!
Managing users, roles, and data protection might sound like technical territory at first, but as you’ve seen, it’s really about keeping your team productive and your customers’ data safe. With what you’ve learned, you can confidently support your organization’s day-to-day operations while meeting essential privacy standards.
## You’re now able to:
  1. Work with users


  * Create and manage user accounts for your team.
  * Update user details like passwords and usernames.
  * Invite new colleagues into your Xperience project.


  1. Set up roles and permissions


  * Understand how role-based access works.
  * Create roles that match your team’s responsibilities.
  * Adjust permissions so everyone has access to the right tools.
  * Create a special role for a Data Protection Officer.


  1. Protect customer data


  * Understand how Xperience supports GDPR and privacy compliance.
  * Find out what personal data you collected about a contact.
  * Manage customer consent and respond to access requests.
  * Handle deletion requests securely and correctly.


With these skills, you’re helping to keep your team focused, your tools secure, and your business compliant. If you ever need a refresher, please return to this module anytime.
