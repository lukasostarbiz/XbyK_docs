You’ll need a demo Kbank website to follow up on the exercises. If you don’t have your demo site, request the [Xperience by Kentico - Business Tutorial Kbank demo site](https://www.kentico.com/services/training/training-website-requests) through a form at [Kentico website](https://www.kentico.com/services/training/training-website-requests).
