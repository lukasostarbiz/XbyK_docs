# Upgrade to Xperience by Kentico
Ready to modernize your digital experience platform? This section provides comprehensive guidance for upgrading your existing projects to Xperience by Kentico.
Whether you’re a team lead planning the upgrade, a solution architect working through the migration, or a developer looking for advanced technical guidance, here you’ll find resources tailored to your needs.
