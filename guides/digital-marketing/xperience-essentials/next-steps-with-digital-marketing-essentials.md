# Next steps with digital marketing
You’ve come to the end of the **Digital marketing essentials** guide. At this point, you have basic knowledge and understanding of how the different Xperience by Kentico capabilities help you capture and store data about your website visitors on your Xperience websites, and how to leverage that data into actionable insights.
