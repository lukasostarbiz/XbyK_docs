Welcome to the **Business user learning map for Xperience by Kentico**. This curated collection of training materials, documentation guides, and additional resources is designed to guide business users – such as marketers, content editors, and administrators – through the key areas of our [**digital experience platform** (**DXP**).](https://www.kentico.com/discover/what-is-a-dxp)

Each section is organized by topic and includes links to recommended resources, guiding you step by step – without overwhelming you with technical detail.
## Xperience by Kentico DXP overview
Xperience by Kentico is a flexible _digital experience platform_ that empowers business users to manage content, personalize customer experiences, and run digital marketing activities – all in one place. Whether you’re creating web content, mapping out customer journeys, or configuring user access and workflows, Xperience gives you the tools to deliver impactful customer experiences.
We recommend starting with content management and digital marketing essentials in Xperience. These core capabilities provide a solid foundation for working with more advanced features – and will help you make informed decisions that support your business goals.

Strong content management turns ideas into experiences. From web pages to files and multilingual content, your work as a marketer or editor directly **shapes what your visitors see and how they feel**. This section introduces the essential tools in _Xperience by Kentico_ that help you create, organize, reuse, and publish content efficiently across channels. Whether you’re building a simple landing page or managing an entire multilingual site, these guides will give you the confidence and clarity to get started.

## Digital marketing essentials
Xperience lets you capture and store visitor data, transform it into actionable insights, and tailor experiences across different marketing channels.
To connect meaningfully with your audience, you need to **understand who your visitors are, how they behave, and what kind of content resonates with them**. That’s where digital marketing in _Xperience by Kentico_ comes in. These tools help you collect data, track how customers interact with your brand, and create personalized experiences – so you can run smarter campaigns and build stronger relationships.
The resources in this section teach you how to manage contact data, segment audiences, personalize website content, and more. These capabilities form the backbone of data-driven marketing in Xperience, giving you everything you need to stop guessing and start driving conversions.

## Customer journeys
Today’s customers expect more than just relevant content – they expect **meaningful experiences** delivered **at the right time, on the right channel**. To meet these expectations, marketers need a way to design, automate, and optimize the entire customer lifecycle.
That’s where customer journeys come in.
The **Customer Journeys** application in Xperience helps you see how people interact with your brand, identify critical touchpoints, and create smoother paths that lead visitors from awareness to action. By guiding your audience through personalized experiences, you can drive conversions, increase loyalty, and support every stage of the customers’ journey – from first visit to long-term retention.
You’ll learn how to create and manage individual journeys, guide leads through the funnel, and deliver tailored experiences that boost engagement and loyalty. Happy customers make for happy ROI results.
### Kentico-specific customer journey resources
  1. [Get started with customer journeys](guides/customer-journeys/get-started-with-customer-journeys) – Learn how to analyze customer behavior to improve user engagement and increase conversions.
  2. [Anatomy of customer journeys – Key components](guides/customer-journeys/anatomy-of-customer-journeys-key-components) – Discover the essential building blocks of a customer journey, including journey **stages** , **touchpoints** , and **customer segments**.
  3. [Prepare to build customer journey](guides/customer-journeys/prepare-to-build-customer-journey) – Find out which internal teams should be involved and what resources you need to define and shape effective customer journeys.
  4. [Customer journeys](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/customer-journeys) – Use the tools available in Xperience to put your knowledge into practice and build a customer journey of your own.
  5. [Work with contacts](guides/digital-marketing/work-with-contacts) – Learn how to manage contacts and contact groups and where to view each contact’s customer journey history.
  6. [Activity tracking](guides/digital-marketing/activity-tracking) – Learn how to track visitor actions – such as page views and content consumption – to better understand how visitors move through your customer journeys.


## User management essentials
Effective user and role management is key to maintaining secure, structured, and efficient collaboration within _Xperience by Kentico_.
Whether you’re part of a large team or managing a few contributors, user and role management ensures that the **right people have access to the right tools** – without compromising security. In Xperience, you can easily invite new team members, define their responsibilities, and maintain compliance with data protection regulations. Understanding how to manage users and roles will encourage a well-organized environment where everyone can work efficiently and safely. You’re protecting not only yourself but others, too.
### Kentico-specific user management resources
  1. [User management introduction](guides/digital-marketing/xperience-essentials/user-management-essentials-introduction) – This short series will guide you through the essentials—signing in, creating and managing users, and defining roles for a well-organized and secure environment.
  2. [*Work with roles](guides/digital-marketing/xperience-essentials/work-with-roles-essentials) – Learn how to use the **Role management** application to set up roles, assign permissions, and control access to different areas of the system.
  3. [Work with users](guides/digital-marketing/xperience-essentials/work-with-users-essentials) – Get comfortable with the **Users** application. This guide shows you how to create or edit a user and update your password.
  4. [Work with users and roles](guides/configuration/work-with-users-and-roles) – Take a deeper dive into managing users and roles. Learn how to invite users, assign them roles, reset passwords, or even set up a data protection officer role.
  5. [Data protection](guides/configuration/data-protection) – Explore how to manage personal data, customer consent, and data removal requests in compliance with regulations like GDPR. This guide walks you through locating, sharing, and deleting customer data using Xperience’s **Data Protection** feature.


## Advanced topics for business users
Ready to explore more? This section gathers helpful tools and features that go **beyond the basics**. These guides are great for business users who want to fine-tune their content, organize files more effectively, experiment with new publishing options, or simply get more confident working in Xperience.
Even if some topics seem more advanced, don’t worry – each topic is designed to help you take things step by step, with practical examples and clear instructions along the way.
### Kentico-specific resources recommended for business user
  1. [Content hub](documentation/business-users/content-hub) – Go deeper into how the _Content hub_ lets you create, store, manage, and publish content across multiple channels. Learn how to work with **content items** , **content hub folders** , and **content item assets**.
  2. [Taxonomies](guides/digital-marketing/work-with-taxonomies/taxonomies) – Learn how to organize your content with tags and categories to make it easier to filter, personalize, or show relevant content across languages and regions. Give it a try, and learn how to work with taxonomies in Kbank.
  3. [Taxonomies](documentation/developers-and-admins/configuration/taxonomies) (advanced) – Explore how structured tags can power advanced filtering, personalized experiences, and content relationships. Plus, see how developers can retrieve and display tagged content.
  4. [Website content](documentation/business-users/website-content) – Learn how pages work in your website channels, from creating and editing content to managing their life cycle, preview modes, and URLs – all without needing any technical know-how.
  5. [Content versioning](documentation/business-users/content-versioning) – Easily keep track of changes, see who edited what and when, and restore older versions of your content if needed – perfect for catching mistakes or rolling back updates.
  6. [Workflows](documentation/developers-and-admins/configuration/workflows) – Create wokflows, and set up custom review and approval steps to make sure content gets the right eyes on it before publishing. Nothing goes live until it’s truly ready.
  7. [Set up custom workflow notifications](documentation/developers-and-admins/customization/handle-global-events/set-up-custom-workflow-notifications) – Keep your team in the loop with automatic email alerts when content moves through workflow steps – so the right people always know when it’s their turn to review or approve.
  8. [Headless content](documentation/business-users/headless-content) – (Advanced license required) Discover how to manage and publish reusable content for mobile apps, kiosks, and other digital platforms using headless channels – structured content ready to go wherever you need it.
  9. [Rich text editor](documentation/business-users/rich-text-editor) – Format your content with ease using a simple editor that lets you style text, add images or links, and even switch to HTML view if needed – what you see really is what you get!
  10. [Email queue](documentation/business-users/email-queue) – Keep track of system emails like password resets. View what’s been sent, spot any that failed, and resend them if needed, all from one place.
  11. [Members](documentation/business-users/members) – Manage visitor accounts that sign up on your site. View their details, enable or disable access, or remove accounts when needed.
  12. If you’re keen on keeping up with the times and trends, look no further. We’ve developed AIRA – our native Artificial Intelligence Recommendations and Assistance capability – to help boost productivity and gain insights. Let’s dig in!
    1. [AIRA is here](https://www.kentico.com/platforms/xperience-by-kentico/digital-marketing/aira) – Meet AIRA – your AI-powered teammate designed to speed up content creation, automate repetitive tasks, and deliver insights that help you get better results, faster. From writing emails to optimizing images, AIRA helps marketers work smarter across digital channels.
    2. [AIRA: Your digital marketing buddy](https://www.kentico.com/discover/blog/aira-digital-marketing) – AIRA is your always-on AI teammate that helps you work smarter – not harder. From creating multichannel content and optimizing campaigns to making data-driven decisions, AIRA takes care of time-consuming tasks so you can focus on strategy, creativity, and results.
    3. [How AI is revolutionizing email personalization](https://www.kentico.com/discover/blog/ai-email-personalization) – Discover how AI is changing the way marketers create emails, making them more relevant, timely, and effective. From dynamic content and smart automation to real-time insights, learn how tools like AIRA help you deliver email experiences your audience actually wants to open.
    4. [AIRA](documentation/business-users/aira) – (Advanced license required) Try out AIRA, your AI-powered content agent that helps you save time by refining text, generating email content, and tagging or describing images. Currently in preview and evolving with your feedback.
