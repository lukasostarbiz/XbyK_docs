# Customer journeys
**Advanced license required**   
  
Features described on this page require the Xperience by Kentico **Advanced** license tier. 
Welcome to the **customer journeys** series. Here, you’ll find guides to help you understand and use the _Customer journeys_ feature in your project.
You’ll learn how to create and manage individual journeys, guide leads through the funnel, and deliver tailored experiences that drive conversions and engagement.
Are you a marketer looking to deliver more thoughtful, more personalized experiences? Then this series of guides is for you.
## What are customer journeys?
Today’s customers expect more than just relevant content – they expect meaningful experiences at the right time on the right channel. To meet these expectations, marketers need a way to design, automate, and optimize the entire customer lifecycle.
That’s where customer journeys come in.
## Orchestrating customer journeys with Xperience by Kentico
_Customer journeys_ in Xperience by Kentico help you map out the end-to-end experience for your audiences – from their first interaction with your brand to long-term loyalty. You can automate steps along the journey, personalize actions based on real-time data, and optimize for better outcomes.
Whether you’re nurturing leads, onboarding new customers, or re-engaging existing ones, _Customer journeys_ give you the tools to guide each person through the right path every step of the way.
Explore this tutorial to learn how to turn audience insights into action – and journeys into results.
