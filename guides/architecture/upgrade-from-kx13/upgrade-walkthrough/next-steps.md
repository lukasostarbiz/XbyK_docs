# Next steps
Congrats! You have successfully migrated a whole Dancing Goat page from **Kentico Xperience 13** to **Xperience by Kentico**
What’s next?
## Continue learning
Now that you have the foundation to build on top of, we recommend exploring the upgrade process for the features and capabilities used in your project.
You may find our [KX13 and XbyK capability comparison guide](/guides/architecture/upgrade-from-kx13/plan-your-strategy-for-migrating-features) helpful in determining your upgrade strategy.
Find more information on the migration process in [our documentation](/documentation/developers-and-admins/upgrade-to-xperience-by-kentico) and detailed information on our ever-evolving Kentico Migration Toolkit in the [Xperience by Kentico: Kentico Migration Tool](https://github.com/Kentico/xperience-by-kentico-kentico-migration-tool/tree/master) repository.
## Let us know what you’re missing
We are continuously tuning our existing materials and adding new guides. Use the **Send us feedback** button at the end of this page to let us know what parts of the upgrade you would like more guidance or code samples on.
