# Anatomy of customer journeys - Key components
**Advanced license required**   
  
Features described on this page require the Xperience by Kentico **Advanced** license tier. 
Are you ready to go beyond the basics and start building real, effective customer journeys? This guide breaks it all down.
_Anatomy of customer journeys - Key components_ explores the foundational building blocks every marketer needs to understand to craft journeys that convert – and retain. This article gives you the full picture of what makes a customer journey tick, from understanding how customers move through different **stages** (**See, Think, Do, Care**) to mapping each interaction across **touchpoints** and **customer segments**.
In this guide, you’ll learn:
  * What touchpoints are – and how they shape the customer’s experience.
  * How to identify and work with customer segments like MQLs, SQLs, or loyal customers.
  * Why journey stages matter and how to make sure they reflect what your customers are really thinking.
  * How to work with data integrations to deliver personalized, data-driven experiences.


This is your go-to guide for connecting the dots between strategy and execution – perfect for marketers looking to deepen their understanding and start building journeys smarter with **Xperience by Kentico**.
