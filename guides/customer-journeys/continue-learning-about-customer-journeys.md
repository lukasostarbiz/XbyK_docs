# Continue learning about customer journeys
**Advanced license required**   
  
Features described on this page require the Xperience by Kentico **Advanced** license tier. 
You’ve come far in your path towards understanding **Customer journeys**.
  * You’ve learned about the digital marketing struggles that marketers face daily, and you’ve also learned how Xperience by Kentico can help by using the integrated _Customer journeys_ feature.
  * You revisited and learned how to distinguish core concepts, from customer and buyer’s journeys, to user stories, and the overarching notion of the sales funnel.
  * You now understand how impactful customer journeys can be when their capabilities are leveraged right, becoming a powerful tool to have in your digital marketing toolbox.


Even so, considering the investment into _Customer journeys_ against their value may sound like a lot at this point – and this is why we prepared a safe sandbox area for you to test their merits in action.
You can test all your marvellous new ideas on our hosted demo website – **Kbank**. Why not explore for example these following features:
  * **Digital marketing** - especially the applications **Contact groups, Contact management, Automation,** and **Forms** will be instrumental to creating a customer journey.
  * **Configuration** - see how you can handle customers’ data in our **Data protection** series.


Request access to a demo Kbank website via the [form at kentico.com.](https://www.kentico.com/services/training/training-website-requests) (Select the Xperience by Kentico – Business Tutorial Kbank demo site option.)

  * [Data protection](/guides/configuration/data-protection) – See how you can manage personal data or customer consents.

  * [Work with contacts](/guides/digital-marketing/work-with-contacts) – Learn how to manage contacts and contact groups, and where to see your contact’s customer journey.
  * [Work with forms](/guides/digital-marketing/work-with-forms) – See how to work with forms, adjust their fields, and make them user-friendly for website visitors to improve their user experience.
