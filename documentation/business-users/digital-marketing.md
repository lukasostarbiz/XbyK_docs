The digital marketing features in Xperience by Kentico enable you to attract new visitors, promote your business and products, analyze traffic and visitor activities, and keep in touch with existing contacts and customers.
## Manage marketing features
As a marketer or content editor, you can use the built-in digital marketing tools to:
  * Get a centralized [overview of visitors (contacts)](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/contact-management) who view your websites or access content through other channels
  * Track [activities](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/contact-activities) your contacts perform and evaluate their behavior
  * Set up [automation processes](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/automation) that interact with your website’s visitors (e.g., email follow-ups after a visitor submits a form).
  * Segment contacts into [contact groups](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/contact-groups), based on rules and conditions
  * Create [customer journeys](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/customer-journeys) to observe contact interactions with your content
  * Create [forms](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/forms) to collect feedback and other data from visitors
  * Create and send [emails](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/emails)
    * Send emails via autoresponders to visitors who submit a [form](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/forms/create-and-edit-forms)
    * Set up subscription forms to build [recipient lists](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/emails/send-regular-emails-to-subscribers) for your emails
    * Track and evaluate [statistics](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/emails/track-email-statistics) for your emails
  * Personalize widgets to display different content based on conditions: 
    * [Page Builder widget personalization](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/widget-personalization)
    * [Email Builder widget personalization](documentation/business-users/digital-marketing/emails/create-emails-in-email-builder#personalize-widgets)
